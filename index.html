Bus Delays 2020-2022

From 2020 to 2022, the years the fall in the heart of the pandemic, school bus delays were because many people were at home and employed which subjected kids to online learning and that meant less of a need for school buses since hardly any children when going to school in person for a while. The borough with 13,994 instances of delays, the highest number of bus delays throughout these years was Manhattan as seen in the chart below which can be attributed to more people leaving Manhattan and going to other areas during the pandemic.

Bus Delay 2022-2024

In the years coming out of the pandemic to now, bus delays in each borough have more than doubled due to the resume of normal activity after the pandemic. Now the borough with the highest number of school bus delays is the Bronx, with 32,713 delays. New technology is needed to improve the bus routes for students said Sam Catalinatto, co-founder of the advocacy group Parents to Improve School Transportation, in a Gothamist article. This can be attributed to the bus driver shortage in the Bronx, meaning that it takes longer for children in the Bronx to get picked up from school.
